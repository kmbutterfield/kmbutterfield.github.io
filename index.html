Welcome...

Hi, I'm Kim Butterfield. I'm a technology fanatic, an ex-web and graphics designer, photographer, bass guitarist, and always will be a Rubik's cube speed solver. This is my online academic portfolio; on this website, you will find links to my portfolio of work and CV.

I'm currently studying for my MA in Social Research Methods (Interdisciplinary) at the University of Leeds, funded by an ESRC WRDTP 1+3 Studentship. In collaboration with Sainsbury's Ltd. I will begin my PhD in 2018 in the School of geography. My thesis focuses on developing an agent-based model that incorporates consumer store and channel choice behaviours at the individual level. My key areas of academic interest are retail geography, agent-based modelling, demographic projections, coding, and simulating human behaviours.

When I'm not in glass getting my head around Python and sociological terminology, you can find me slashing zombies on virtual reality, playing The Sims franchise, or learning about the latest and greatest pieces of mobile technology.
